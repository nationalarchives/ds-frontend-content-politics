Advertisement for a dinner in Gateshead in August 1830 to celebrate the July 1830 revolution in France
(Catalogue ref: HO 44/21)

THE
Friends of Liberty
Will dine at Mr. Kenmir’s,
Geo. III Inn, Gateshead, on
Tuesday, Aug. 24th, 1830,
IN
COMMEMORATION
OF THE RECENT
GLORIUS TRIUMPH OVER
TYRANNY IN FRANCE.
Dinner on the Table at 3 o’ Clock in the Afternoon.
Tickets to be had at Mr Kenmir’s Bar, and W. Stephenson’s
Shop, No 3, Bridge End, Gateshead – Price 5s. 6d.

 Transcript
tyranny

government where a single ruler has total power; total power used unjustly, harshly or cruelly
